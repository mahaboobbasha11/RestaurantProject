    Walking toward Cafe Noir Bangalore, you feel like there’s an invisible portal somewhere that takes you directly to the streets of Paris. Cozy cafes dot the streets of Paris, making it one of the most romantic cities in the world. At Cafe Noir, you are transported to this city of love complete with food, ambiance, and music.

    Cafe noir is a contemporary French casual café chain serving authentic French casual cuisine from breakfast to dinner.

    The main objective of the brand is to promote the French “Art De Vivre” with a wide selection of gourmet foods in a distinctive bistro feel that embraces the true french culture.

    From a sweet treat to an elaborate gourmet meal, the restaurants have specialties to suit everyone’s palate.
